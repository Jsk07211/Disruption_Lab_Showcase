Redrock

Problem

Ineffective Screening Process:

Inaccurate Profiling

Labour-Intensive

Our Solution

A ML System for Two-Part Risk-Flagging:

Stats-Based

ML Model draws upon credit bureau data and past customer behaviour to predict risk score

Notes-Based

ML Model draws upon staff reports to predict risk score

Lower Risk
